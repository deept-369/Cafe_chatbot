Our Story

Born From a Love
of Good Coffee

Founded in 2015 by Maya and Jordan, Brewed Awakening started as a tiny corner shop with a single espresso machine and a big dream. Today, we're Brooklyn's beloved third space — where remote workers, neighbours, and coffee purists all find their place.

We source beans directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, and roast them in-house every Thursday. Every cup tells a story.
